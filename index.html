WHAT IS SKULLS LUDO ?

Skulls Ludo is one of the most popular board games across the world and chances are that you would have played at some time or other. Play to Earn is flipping the gaming industry on its head so now for the first-time players will be able to play ludo in metaverse with best Play-to-Earn experience. We have built our blockchain game skulls ludo around haunting skull face NFT art, in which one skull is wearing soldier hat or a cigar in the mouth, they all have found themselves here. Only 10,000 skulls will ever exist. All skulls are unique and there will never be another one like it. Record of them is stored on the Solana Blockchain.
Players use their NFTs as their characters in skulls ludo game. GAME MODES

Skulls Ludo comes with two modern game modes

ISLAND OF SKULLS

Island of skulls is a modern way of playing ludo board game where you just have to take one puck from the base to the home. The faster you move your puck to the base the more you are near to win the game.

GUIDE RULES

SEA OF SKULLS

Sea of skulls is a traditional way of playing ludo board game where you have to take all the four pucks from the base to home in order to win the game by keeping yourself in the limits of the rules.
