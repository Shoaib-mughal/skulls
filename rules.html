TWO MODERN GAME MODES

Island of Skulls

Island of Skulls is a modern way of playing ludo board game where you just have to take one puck from the base to the home. The faster you move your puck to the base the more you are near to win the game.

Rules:

  • ◉ You just need to score only one puck to win the game.
  • ◉ Capturing a puck is not necessary to enter into the stretch of a home.
  • ◉ Rolling a 6 will make puck get out of the base.
  • ◉ If 6 is being rolled three times in a row then it will cancel the turn.
  • ◉ Joint tokens cannot be captured at any cost.
  • ◉ Undo option will re-rolls the outcome of last dice.
  • ◉ You will get an additional roll once you have rolled 6, captured a puck of other player, or scored a puck.
  • ◉ Pucks that are stopped at the safe point cannot be captured.

Sea of Skulls

Sea of Skulls is traditional way of playing ludo board game where you have to take all the four pucks from the base to home in order to win the game by keeping yourself in the limits of the rules.

Rules:

  • ◉ You need to score all four pucks to win the game.
  • ◉ Capturing a puck is not necessary to enter into the stretch of a home.
  • ◉ Rolling a 6 will make puck get out of the base.
  • ◉ If 6 is being rolled three times in a row then it will cancel the turn.
  • ◉ Joint tokens cannot be captured at any cost.
  • ◉ Undo option will re-rolls the outcome of last dice.
  • ◉ You will get an additional roll once you have rolled 6, captured a puck of other player, or scored a puck.
  • ◉ Pucks that are stopped at the safe point cannot be captured.
